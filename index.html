I am a renaissance artist with work across poetry, nonfiction, painting, art installations and beautiful objects. I can't seem to settle on a medium or a topic: I spend one summer writing poetry about the sensation of consciousness expanding and the next making giant paintings of jellyfish. I make a living as a jack-of-all-trades engineer, touching mechanical, electrical, software, and data engineering. I think about what it would mean for a computer to 'interpret' poetry.
