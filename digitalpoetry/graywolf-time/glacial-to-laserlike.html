The poem comes to you in a dream, on the bus, while staring at the wall. It emerges, becomes familiar. The words become your own. That turn of phrase, that perfect image. Seared into your mind. Then, you're not paying attention, you're looking elsewhere---it leaves. You forget a word or two, a line, the volta, what was it? The poem recedes. You don't recognize it anymore.

Try to see your poem like a glacier does. Or see it on the timescale of a beam of light. The only important thing is to see it anew.
