"36 Views of the Sea" is a series of anagrammatical poems by Katy Ilonka Gero, each of which uses all and only the letters from an Etel Adan poem about the sea. All poems in the series are therefore also anagrams of each other. This piece, with poems 4 and 6 in the series, attempts to show the anagrammatical nature of the poems.
